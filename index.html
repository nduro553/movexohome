Our Installation Process

Step 1: Initial Consultation and Measurement

We begin every project with a thorough consultation to understand your needs, preferences, and budget considerations. Our team visits your location to take precise measurements and assess the existing conditions. We discuss material options, edge profiles, and any special requirements you may have. This initial phase is crucial for planning a successful installation.

Step 2: Template Creation and Approval

Using advanced templating techniques, we create exact patterns of your countertop areas. This includes all cutouts for sinks, cooktops, and other fixtures. We review the templates with you to ensure everything meets your expectations before proceeding to fabrication. This step prevents costly mistakes and ensures perfect fit.

Step 3: Professional Installation

Our certified installers arrive with all necessary equipment and materials. We carefully position and secure your new countertops, ensuring level surfaces and proper support. All seams are expertly joined, and cutouts are precisely executed. We handle plumbing reconnections and make final adjustments for perfect alignment.

Step 4: Sealing and Finishing

For natural stone installations, we apply appropriate sealers to protect your investment. All edges are polished to perfection, and we clean the entire work area thoroughly. We conduct a final inspection with you to ensure complete satisfaction with the installation quality and appearance.

Step 5: Care Instructions and Follow-up

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical countertop installation take?

Most standard kitchen countertop installations are completed in one day. The exact timeline depends on the size of the project, complexity of the layout, and number of cutouts required. Bathroom vanity installations typically take a few hours. We provide specific time estimates during the consultation phase based on your particular project requirements.

Do I need to remove my old countertops before installation?

We can handle the removal of existing countertops as part of our service. Our team safely removes old countertops and disposes of them properly. We also inspect the cabinet structure during removal to ensure it's ready to support new countertops. If you prefer to remove old countertops yourself, we can provide guidance on proper removal techniques.

What preparation is needed before installation day?

We recommend clearing countertop areas and removing items from cabinets below the installation area. Disconnect any appliances that will be affected by the installation. Ensure clear access to the work area for our team and materials. We'll provide a detailed preparation checklist during the consultation to ensure smooth installation day proceedings.

Can you install countertops over existing cabinets?

Yes, in most cases we can install new countertops over existing cabinets. However, we first assess the cabinet condition and structural integrity to ensure they can support the weight of new countertops, especially heavy materials like granite or marble. If reinforcement is needed, we can provide that service as well.

How do you handle seams in countertop installations?

Seams are sometimes necessary for larger countertop areas or specific layouts. We strategically place seams in less visible locations when possible. Our craftsmen use color-matched epoxy and precise joining techniques to create tight, nearly invisible seams. We discuss seam placement during the templating phase to ensure optimal aesthetic results.

What maintenance do installed countertops require?

Maintenance requirements vary by material. Natural stone countertops benefit from periodic sealing to maintain stain resistance. Quartz surfaces require minimal maintenance beyond regular cleaning. We provide detailed care instructions specific to your countertop material, including recommended cleaning products and maintenance schedules to keep your countertops looking beautiful for years.
